
First off, run:
# autoreconf -f -i

Then the usual ./configure and make

In order to build gss-proxy the following development packages are needed:

    autoconf
    automake
    docbook-style-xsl
    doxygen
    findutils
    gettext-devel
    iniparser-devel
    keyutils-libs-devel
    krb5-devel
    libselinux-devel
    libtool
    libverto-devel
    libxml2
    libxslt
    m4
    pkgconfig
    popt-devel
